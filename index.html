About KrishiGyan

KrishiGyan is an AI-powered platform designed to assist farmers in making informed decisions about crop selection. By analyzing soil type, real-time weather data, and market demand trends, we provide the best crop recommendations to maximize yield and profit.

Our mission is to revolutionize agriculture through technology, ensuring sustainable farming and food security for future generations.

How to Use the Tool

1

Select your Soil Type from the dropdown menu.

2

Enter your Weather Condition based on your region.

3

Provide the Market Demand for better crop selection.

4

Click on Get Recommendation to receive AI-based crop suggestions.
